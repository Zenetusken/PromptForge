# PromptForge Brand Identity System

This document is a narrative summary for onboarding and external communication. If the two documents ever conflict, the Brand Guidelines are authoritative.

---

## The Core Idea

PromptForge is a **digital forge** — a place where raw, imprecise language is heated, shaped, and tempered into precision-engineered instructions. The metaphor is industrial-alchemical: you bring crude material in, a structured pipeline transforms it, and what comes out is measurably sharper. The name fuses *Prompt* (the input domain) and *Forge* (transformation through fire and pressure).

The tagline — **"AI-Powered Prompt Optimization"** — is deliberately technical and direct. No marketing fluff. It tells you exactly what it does.

## Visual Identity

**Dark-first, absolute flat-neon contours.** There are ZERO effects that simulate diffuse light — no soft shadows, no bloom, no radiance, no halo. The word "glow" is banned from code, comments, and specs. Depth and interaction are communicated through *razor-sharp, solid neon borders and color fills*. The aesthetic draws inspiration from precisely bent neon tube signage: clean, geometric, flat.

This inverts the typical light-mode paradigm. Instead of "closer to the light source = more elevated," here it's "sharper contour emission = more interactive." Every interactive state (hover, focus, active) is expressed as a *solid geometric neon border or pure color fill*.

**Ultra-compact density.** The UI follows VS Code density with Excel's data-rich information hierarchy. Information density is a feature, not a bug. Padding, gaps, and radii skew toward the smaller end of each scale. Most panels use 8px padding. Radii of 10px+ are reserved for CTA buttons and rare dialog containers.

**Chromatic encoding.** 10 neon signal colors on a 6-tier dark background. Every strategy, task type, and complexity level has a unique color assignment. Color *is* data, not decoration.

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Cyberpunk** | Near-black surfaces, sharp neon contours, glass morphism, terminal aesthetic. A tool for operators, not a consumer product. |
| **Intelligent** | Communicates through metrics: scores, strategy names, confidence percentages, pipeline stages. "8.2/10 on clarity" not "great job." |
| **Precise** | Numeric scoring (1–10), structured 4-stage pipeline, named strategies with formal frameworks. Nothing is vague. |
| **Bold** | High-contrast neon palette, solid gradients, pure neon UI elements. Sharp 1px borders and solid color fills. |
| **Technical** | Monospace typography for data. Developer-first vocabulary: "forge" not "submit," "pipeline" not "workflow," "strategy" not "method." |

## The Gradient Signature

The brand's most recognizable visual element is the **cyan-to-purple gradient** (`#00e5ff → #7c3aed → #a855f7` at 135 degrees). It appears on primary buttons and hero text. This gradient represents the transformation arc — from raw (cyan, electric, unrefined) to optimized (purple, processed, elevated).

## Voice & Tone

The brand speaks like a confident instrument panel — technical over emotional, precise over vague, concise over explanatory. Canon vocabulary: **Forge**, **Strategy**, **Pipeline**, **Score**, **Prompt**. Banned alternatives: submit, method, workflow, rating, query. Visual effects vocabulary: **contour** (not glow), **flash** (not pulse), **tint** (not halo).

---

**In summary**: PromptForge's brand identity is *industrial cyberpunk precision tooling*. It's a forge, not a playground. The visual language communicates through sharp neon emission on dark fields, chromatic data encoding, monospace numerics, and spring-loaded motion. The voice is that of a confident, data-driven instrument — never casual, never vague. Every design decision traces back to the core metaphor: raw material goes in, measured transformation happens through a named pipeline, and something quantifiably better comes out.
